Features

One rule everywhere: the numbers must not lie.

Net worth = the sum of your account balances. Transfers leave it unchanged, income raises it, expense lowers it. Everything else follows.

Honest accounts

Cash, Bank & Card with real balances. UPI and debit cards spend their bank's money — a payment is never counted twice.

Income · Expense · Transfer

Three transaction types kept strictly apart. Transfers never pollute budgets or reports.

Budgets that speak up

Per-category budgets with period windows, live progress, and alerts at 80% and on overspend.

Dues & loans

Who owes what, partial settlements, running balances. Lending is not spending — dues stay out of your budgets.

Calendar & reminders

Day-wise money-in / money-out grid. EMI and bill reminders that post nothing until you confirm.

Insights

Category breakdown, income vs expense, net-worth trend and per-account reports.

Backup & export

One-tap JSON backup and restore, plus CSV export shaped for accountants and Tally.

Bank-SMS capturesoon

Paused in 1.1 for Play compliance. The on-device pipeline — parser, dedupe, review cards, undo — is ready and waiting.

Privacy

The app can't leak your data. It has no internet.

Release builds don't request the INTERNET permission, so transmitting your data is technically impossible — not a promise, a property. Verify it yourself: the code is open.

No INTERNET permission No SMS permission No analytics No account No cloud
