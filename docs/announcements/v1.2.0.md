# XPENC 1.2.0 — subcategories, dues on the dashboard, every currency, and one-tap import

XPENC 1.2.0 is out. It's the first **feature** release since 1.1.0, and it's a
big one — four things people asked for, all offline, all on your device.

## What's new

**🗂️ Subcategories.** Group your spending: put *Groceries* and *Restaurants*
under *Food*. A subcategory's spend rolls up into its parent across the
dashboard, Stats and reports, and a budget on a parent covers the whole group.
Manage them in **More → Categories**.

**🤝 Dues & owes on the dashboard.** A new **People** section shows what you'll
get and what you'll pay at a glance, over everyone with an outstanding balance —
because money you've lent is still yours, not an expense.

**🌍 Every world currency.** Pick from ~90 currencies — **Bangladeshi Taka
included** — in **Settings → Currency**. Amounts reformat everywhere instantly.
Currency symbol missing? Flip **Show currency symbol** off and see clean numbers.

**📤 Import — move to a new phone in two taps.** You could already export your
data; now you can bring it back in. **More → Backup & Restore → Import from
file** loads a backup from anywhere — including one carried over from another
phone. Export on the old phone, import on the new one, done. A safety copy is
saved before every import, and a wrong file changes nothing.

## Upgrading

**Your data is safe.** Installing over your existing app keeps everything — the
database migrates itself in place, no reinstall needed. Still, **export a backup
first** (*More → Download Data → Export JSON*) — one minute of insurance.

One caveat: if you install a build signed with a *different key* than the one on
your phone, Android forces an uninstall (which wipes data). Match the source, or
restore your backup afterwards. Full details in the
[Upgrade Guide](https://github.com/PATILYASHH/XPENC/blob/master/wiki/Upgrade-Guide.md).
